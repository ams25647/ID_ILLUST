About Me

I have been drawing since I can remember, but I started getting more into the hobby of drawing around the 7th grade when I was hanging around different artists that were also into the same things I was in. At around the end of my high school years I really wanted to see if I could take my work to the next level and started practicing everyday working on different parts of areas, researching many different things and reading books from my favorite artists and seeing what they did that made them as good as they are today. Although I still have much to learn and much to practice on, I will be using this website as a journal or blog to showcase my experience with drawing multiple different areas in art, ranging from creating album artwork to working on my own short story comic. Each piece goes more into detail about the thought process of diving into each project, from the background research I did before to going into drawing up rough drafts of my work.
